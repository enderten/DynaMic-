# 🏝️ DynaMic (Liquid Glass Edition)

> "Code is art, performance is everything."

**DynaMic** is a high-performance native macOS tool designed to bring the **Dynamic Island** experience to devices running **macOS 12 Monterey** and newer. Focused on **Liquid Glass** aesthetics and efficiency, it is optimized for Apple Silicon (M1/M2) and classic Intel Macs (MacBook Pro 2015 approved).

---

## ✨ Features

- **Liquid Glass UI:** Real-time glass morphism effects with frosted borders and dynamic glow.
- **DynaMic Shapes:** Choose between **Island** (Capsule), **Punch Hole** (Circle), or **Larger** (Notch) to match your hardware.
- **M2 Optimized:** Near 0% CPU impact with fluid spring-physics (elastic) animations.
- **Auto-Boot Support:** Reliable "Launch at Login" for macOS 12 (via AppleScript) and macOS 13+ (via SMAppService).
- **Dual Language:** Full native support for English and Spanish.
- **Quick Controls:** Access customization, position sliders, and power options directly from the Menu Bar.

---

## 🚀 Installation & Usage

1. **Download:** Go to the [Releases](https://github.com/enderten/DynaMic/releases) section and download the latest `DynaMic.zip`.
2. **Extract:** Open the zip file and move `DynaMic.app` to your **Applications** folder.
3. **Open:** Right-click the app and select **Open** (to bypass the macOS unidentified developer warning).
4. **Enjoy:** Use the menu bar icon to customize your Liquid Island position and style!

> **Note for macOS 12 users:** If "Launch at Login" doesn't trigger, ensure you've granted the app permission to send Apple Events in *System Preferences > Security & Privacy*.
